A solution for every saver

We're on a mission to make pensions simple so that everyone can enjoy a happy retirement.

Our website and mobile app gives you tools to help manage your private pension, whether you're combining pensions for the first time, or choosing a pension plan that aligns with your values. Through our pension consolidation service, you can bring old pots together, and if you’re self-employed you can start a pension from scratch simply by making a pension contribution. And, if you're over the age of 55 and ready to start making pension withdrawals, you can do that with PensionBee too. Everything’s designed to give you pension confidence - you’ll even get your very own account manager who’ll look after you and your pension every step of the way.

Pick from a range of purpose-built pension plans

Our pension plans are managed by BlackRock, State Street Global Advisors, Legal & General and HSBC - four of the biggest money managers in the world.

Whether you are amalgamating pensions or setting up a self-employed pension, we offer everything from climate-conscious impact investing to pension plans built to preserve your retirement income. When you consolidate pensions with us, you can pick a plan or we'll simply invest you in Tailored, our most popular option, which automatically moves your money into safer assets as you grow older. Across each of our plans we charge one simple annual pension fee and you can change your plan at any time.
